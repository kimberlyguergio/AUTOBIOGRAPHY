My Childhood

I was born into a very adorable and simple family. Every member of my family is very caring and loving. I have three siblings, of which two are brothers and one is a sister. We all used to play together with my friends. I clearly remember all the games we used to play, like Patentero, Luksong Baka, Luksong Lubid, Chinese Garter, Baril-Barilan, etc. In childhood, there was not a huge list of duties to do. We could do whatever we wanted. One of the memories I will never forget is flying kites near the seashore. I'm happy if the air is strong because the stronger the air, the higher the kite will fly. I'm so glad that I can experience games that the next generation can't because of these emerging technologies. There is no doubt that childhood is the best part of one's life.

My Teenage Life

My life as a teenager is so wonderful. Though sometimes I experience doubts, and some teenagers will say that my life is boring, I'm very sure that I'm on the right track because my parents always guide me. Taking their advice will help me understand myself better. It helped me become a better person.I'm proud of myself for all the things that I have been through and the challenges that I have overcome. I'm thinking and analyzing everything to make my life as a teenager as good as ever, with no regrets but good memories to reminisce about when the time comes. Teenage life is the most exciting and memorable experience that one should treasure!

My Adulthood

As an adult, you must be mature and ready to take on the world. This is a stage in life that I was not prepared for and wish could have been avoided. Take me back to being 8 and my toys being the only things I have to worry about. As an adult, I always wanted to be successful. Being successful to me has never meant being rich, for me, it's more about not struggling, having family, and most of all, being happy. Many responsibilities come into play.
